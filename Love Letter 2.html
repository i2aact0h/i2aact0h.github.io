FAITH IN GOD AND MEN

THE LORD GOD ALMIGHTY

Through the years, my parents have taught me a lot of things. I personally thank you, God the Father, Christ Jesus, and the Holy Spirit for bringing me into their lives.
Mom and dad, through my doubts over the years and my questions, you both answered and refered me to wise men and women who helped me strengthed my faith in the Lord. I thank you for
teaching me your faith. The legacy that you have built on Christ for me has become a foundation of which I use to direct my path in life.

MY FELLOW HUMANS

I have both learned to love and hate people. I have realized that we are all capable of both great good and even greater evil. I have lost my faith in my fellow man for sometime.
Mom and dad, thank you for teaching me that we are all sinners and that we humans need to work together to build a better society together. You taught me the value of trust and of the power
of restraint towards the evil and cruel. Thank you for teaching me forgiveness and to trust in the few good men and women left.

Dad, thank you for teching me the value of strength, honor, willpower, wisdom and holding onto promises. You both shaped me to be who I am today.
